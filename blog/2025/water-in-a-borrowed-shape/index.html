I don’t belong anywhere,
I am everywhere — 
But I am never there
Where I need to be.

I am like water in a bottle,
Trapped in a shape that isn’t mine.
Content within someone’s artistic concept — 
But am I even needed in this world?
I surely belong to the sea — 
That’s where I feel my power.

I am the path of light,
Seen, explored — but never chosen.
I am the music of light,
Played but never understood.
Harmonious, yet without a home.
