Every day, the same chase — 
And the chase never ends.
Chasing the rising sun,
Cursing the morning burn.

Chasing the blue moon,
Cursing the afternoon.
Chasing and cursing — 
Without these, life is not lived.

What waits in heaven?
Does the chasing end?
Does the cursing stop?

Yes! someone shouted from the back — 
A drowning sun, with a cold sensation.
A fading moon, with a light burn.
This is heaven, my friend.
