I ask questions — too many questions.
One always remains unanswered — 
What is this sickness?

Questioning my sickness
Becomes the sickness itself.
This light is a sickness.
This life is a sickness.
I don’t know what sickness is — 
Only that life is meant to be lived in it.

Understand it, and you’ll get sick.
Deny it, and sickness will find you on your deathbed.
Ignore it, and maybe you’ll never have it — 
But maybe you never really lived.
