Found love again

Falling in love again,
With the simplicity in her eyes,
The calmness in her nature,
The way she sits near,
The way she walks beside me.

I fell in love again — 
Her beautiful smile,
Her childish giggle,
Her dress,
The way she captures every moment,
Even in the rush of life.

I cannot contain this feeling,
Writing it down, letting the world know:
There’s always a plan,
Nature weaves its threads.
Let’s see if the thread from her finger
Is tied to mine.
I trust nature — 
If she’s meant to be with me, she will be.

I shout to the world:
I’ve discovered the feeling of love.
Watching the sunset from the train,
Her face glowing in the last rays,
Her eyes protected from the light.

I’ve found love and innocence,
In every aspect of life.
