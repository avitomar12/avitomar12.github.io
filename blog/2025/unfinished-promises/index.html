I always cared for only me,
Never thought of what you’d be.
How did you manage to move on?
Like the river that keeps flowing, gone.

How much pain did you endure?
Like a storm that’s fierce, yet pure.
I promised I’d always be near,
But now I’m lost, like leaves, unclear.

Forgot the way that leads me home,
Like the wind, I drift alone.
One thing I’ll always fear,
Is your happiness, my dear.

Your smile, the sun that lights my way,
No matter what, I’ll always stay.
Through everything, like trees that sway,
I could never hate you, come what may.
