Peace! Silence! Quietness!
I’m not sure if these states exist.
Are they real, or just illusions?
Maybe I need a better pen.

Writing slowly, patiently,
Listening to the voice in my head,
Remembering how to write beautifully.

If I believe in you,
Then you must have written my life in haste — 
A question scribbled without care,
Or without caring to ask the question at all.

Why do I curse my life in hard times?
This, too, shall pass,
Just like moments of bliss once did.

I expect nothing, yet I write,
As beautifully as I can — 
Not just to leave the impression
That I had time to write,
But to prove
That something beautiful can still be written.
